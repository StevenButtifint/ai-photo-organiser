Automatically Organise Your Photo Collections using Artificial Intelligence!

Directions

Select the folder containing your unsorted photos. Then click begin and watch your collection get organised into subfolders instantly.

How It Works

Objects, faces and scenery are identified in each image and used to categorise similar images. All operations are performed locally.

Motivation

This application was designed to eliminate the need for manual photo organization after a day out, making it simple to manage and revisit irreplaceable memories.
